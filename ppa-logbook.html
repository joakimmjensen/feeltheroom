Overview

PPA indhold
In this module, I'm going to show you the fastest way to make your new online course available for sale! Here are the steps required: Create your sales page · Create your thank you page · Set up payment processors · Set up checkout · Create your welcome email · Connect all the pieces together *If you're using Kartra, we've set up a lot of this for you and all you have to do is customize ✅ Step 1: A strong sales page is KEY. Fully communicate how incredible your course is and demonstrating how it will help people get from Point A to B. Template provided. Step 2: Thank you page — simple. Confirm purchase + tell them to check email for login info. Step 3: Payment processor — Stripe or PayPal. Start with one, add the other later. Step 4: Set up checkout — in Kartra, add your course as a "product." Step 5: Welcome email — warm welcome confirming purchase, outlining course details, when modules release. Step 6: Connect everything — test the full student journey from sales page through to course access.

Price your Course
Aktiv nu. Pre-sale pris = bedste pris du planlægger at tilbyde. 4 prispunkter: early-bird ét betaling, early-bird plan, regular ét betaling, regular plan.
PPA indhold
Learn the truth about pricing and how to decide on your price point. You want your pre-sale price to be the BEST one that you plan on offering! This is a fantastic deal, available only to those that buy during your 5-day launch (pre-sale promotion). ACTION STEPS — Decide 4 prices: 1. Early-bird pay-in-full 2. Early-bird payment plan 3. Regular price pay-in-full 4. Regular price payment plan Should I offer lifetime access? YES — recommended! It sounds better from a marketing angle, plus people know they can always access it later. Just consider a time container for support (e.g. 90 days or 1 year). On currency: As a business, it's easier to keep all pricing in one currency. Get advice from an accountant on your specific situation.

Set Up Payment Processors
Stripe + PayPal. Stripe anbefales som minimum. PayPal kan have hold på nye konti — normal oplevelse.
PPA indhold
To accept payments online, use a payment processor: Stripe and/or PayPal. Recommendation: Set up both Stripe AND PayPal (you can start with just 1). → If only one: choose Stripe. Why both? Students get more checkout options. Stripe = credit cards easily. PayPal = super easy for anyone to use. ⚠️ New PayPal accounts often have a payment hold — this is unfortunately normal for new or suddenly active accounts. ACTION STEPS: 1. Create Stripe account 2. Create PayPal account 3. Connect PayPal to Kartra 4. Connect Stripe to Kartra

00:00 — ~75s
Start
Hook + the problem. Calm. Direct. No sales tone.
  • I have a hard time relating to other sales coaches or leadership programs
  • I've done SPIN Selling, Solution Selling, MEDDPICC, Situational Leadership, Authentic Leadership
  • They're fucking good at what they do
  • It just doesn't stick
  • Because the F.E.E.L. Foundation is missing
Performance notes
  • "They're fucking good" — neutral. An observation, not an attack.
  • "It just doesn't stick" — pause after. Let them nod inside.
  • "The foundation is missing" — short. Slow. Weight.
  • Thinker energy. Like talking to yourself.
2
01:15 — ~120s
Mid — the F.E.E.L. Framework
The 4 pillars. Take time with each. Use your hands.
  • That's why I built the F.E.E.L. Framework — 13 years distilled into 4 pillars
  • F — Foundation. The Whole Person. You can't give energy you don't have.
  • E — Elevation. Feel The Room. Your state is neurobiologically contagious.
  • E — Exploration. Lead With Curiosity. Stop giving answers — start asking the right questions.
  • L — Liberation. Liberate The Team. Build a culture where people perform because they want to.
Performance notes
  • Use one finger per letter — F, E, E, L. Gives viewer a visual anchor.
  • "You can't give energy you don't have" — pause. Personal. Let it land.
  • "Neurobiologically contagious" — slow. It's a bold claim. Own it.
  • Tease each pillar — they sign up to go deeper.

03:15 — ~75s
Why me
Personal first — credentials last.
  • First of all, because I really believe this is essential — as an individual or team
  • And because I hope you already feel some kind of relation to me
  • I wasn't able to find this person — so I decided I'll become him
  • And then the numbers:
  • 13 years in high-performance sales and leadership across 7 industries
  • Closed million-dollar deals with the biggest companies in Europe
  • Built and scaled 5 different teams with +70 people
  • There aren't many people who've walked the talk and want to teach others how to
Performance notes
  • "I hope you feel some relation" — vulnerable. Don't rush past it.
  • "Become him" — pause before. Two words. Let them land.
  • Credentials: tell, don't sell. Factual, not pitchy.
  • "Walked the talk" — gentle but clear. Your differentiator.
4
04:30 — ~60s
Why you
Honest and filtering. Not for everyone — that's the point.
  • I'm not 100% sure who this will resonate with
  • I only want to work with people who this resonates with
  • I hope I can stand out as a little bit more different than the others
  • But I imagine: other sales leaders, startup founders, sales executives
Performance notes
  • "Not 100% sure who this resonates with" — your strongest line. Honesty beats polish.
  • "Only want people who this resonates" — filters right in, wrong out.
  • No hype. No "this is for YOU if" list. Just honest.
  • Energy: calm, slightly more personal.

05:30 — ~60s
CTA — Request Access
Simple. Concrete. End strong.
  • Below this video (point down)
  • Sign up — tools, frameworks, guides, workshops
  • Dropping every 2nd week
  • Until you hear from me — when I'm ready to work
  • If any of this landed — you know you're the right person
  • Request access. Drop your email. I'll see you on the other side.
Performance notes
  • "Below this video" — point down. Physical direction works.
  • "Until you hear from me" — mystery. Nothing more.
  • "If any of this landed" — pause. Look straight into the lens. This is your close.
  • "I'll see you on the other side" — natural smile. Mean it.
  • Not a sale — an invitation. Hold that energy.

Preflight
1
00:00 — ~75 sec
Start
Hook + problem. Calm. Direct. No sales tone.
I have a hard time relating to other sales coaches or leadership programs
I've done SPIN Selling, Solution Selling, MEDDPICC, Situational Leadership, Authentic Leadership
They're fucking good at what they do
It just doesn't stick
The foundation is missing
Performance
→
"They're fucking good" — neutral. An observation, not an attack.
→
"It just doesn't stick" — pause after. Let them nod inside.
→
"The foundation is missing" — weight. Short. Slow.
→
No salesperson energy. Thinker energy. Like talking to yourself.
2
01:15 — ~120 sec
Mid — the foundations
The core. Take your time. Use your hands.
That's why I've tried to conceptualise my 13 years as a sales executive and leader
Energy as a Compass — Feel the Room
The Whole Human
Build Culture that Builds People — Performative Balance
Lead with questions — on a sales call or in a 1:1
Solution Selling
Performance
→
Count with your fingers — gives the viewer an anchor.
→
"Performative Balance" — say slowly. This is your concept.
→
"The Whole Human" — more personal energy. Eye contact with lens.
→
Tease, don't explain. They sign up to go deeper.
3
03:15 — ~75 sec
Why me
Personal first — credentials last.
First of all, because I really believe this is essential to perform — as individual or team
I hope you already feel some kind of relation to me
I wasn't able to find this person back then — so I decided I'll become him
Then the numbers:
13 years in high-performance sales and leadership across seven industries
Closed million-dollar deals with the biggest companies in Europe
Built and scaled 5 different teams with +70 people
Not many people have walked the talk and want to teach others how to
Performance
→
"I hope you already feel some relation" — vulnerable. Stay there. Don't rush.
→
"I'll become him" — pause before "become him". Two words. Let them land.
→
Credentials: factual, not a pitch. Telling, not selling.
4
04:30 — ~60 sec
Why you
Honest and filtering. Not for everyone — that's a strength.
I'm not 100% sure who this will resonate with
I only want to work with people who this resonates with
I hope I can stand out as a little bit more different than the others
I imagine: sales leaders, startup founders, or sales executives
Performance
→
"Not 100% sure who this will resonate with" — your strongest line. Honesty beats polished pitch.
→
"Only want people who this resonates with" — filters right people in, wrong ones out.
→
No hype. No "this is for YOU if..." list. Just honest observation.
→
Energy: calm and slightly more personal than the rest of the video.
5
05:30 — ~60 sec
CTA — Request Access
Simple. Concrete. End strong.
Below this video (point down)
Sign up → tools, frameworks, guides, workshops
Dropping every 2nd week
Until you hear from me — when I'm ready to work
Once again — if any of this landed, you know you're the right person
Request access. Drop your email. I'll see you on the other side.
Performance
→
"Below this video" — point down with your hand. Physical direction works.
→
"Until you hear from me" — mystery. Nothing more than that.
→
"If any of this landed" — pause before. Look directly into the lens. This is your close.
→
"I'll see you on the other side" — natural smile. Not forced. Mean it.
→
The last words are not a sale — they're an invitation. Hold that energy.

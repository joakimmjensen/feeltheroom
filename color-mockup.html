The method

The F.E.E.L. Framework

Fire principper. To programmer. Én metode der virker for lederen og sælgeren — fordi den samme bevidsthed der gør dig til en god leder, gør dig til en bedre closer.

F
Feel
Feel the room — and yourself. Your energy is neurobiologically contagious. Learn to regulate your state and read your team before you speak.
E
Explore
The one who asks the questions holds the power. Stop giving the answers — start asking the right questions. Dream → Problem → Solution.
E
Elevate
Coaching over instruction. Help your salespeople find the answers themselves. Ask the question that reveals the real blocker.
L
Lead
Build the culture. Recruit the whole person. Create a team that performs because they want to — not because you push them.

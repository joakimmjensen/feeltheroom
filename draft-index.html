The method

The F.E.E.L. Framework

Four principles. Two programs. One method that works for the leader and the salesperson — because the same awareness that makes you a great leader makes you a great closer.

F
Feel
Feel the room — and yourself. Your energy is neurobiologically contagious. Learn to regulate your state, read your salespeople, and build the whole-person foundation that makes everything else possible.
E
Explore
The one who asks the questions holds the power. Stop giving the answers — start asking the right questions. Dream → Problem → Solution. Always in that order.
E
Elevate
Coaching over instruction. Help your salespeople find the answers themselves. Ask the question that reveals the real blocker — and let them hear themselves say what needs to change.
L
Lead
Build the culture. Recruit the whole person. Set targets that motivate. Create the weekly rhythm that keeps the team sharp — and build a team that performs because they want to, not because you push them.
